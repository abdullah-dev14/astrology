What is Space?

Space is a vast, mysterious expanse that begins where the Earth's atmosphere ends. It contains galaxies, stars, planets, and countless wonders yet to be discovered.

Interesting Facts

  • A galaxy is a massive system of stars, stellar remnants, gas, dust, and dark matter, bound together by gravity.
  • There are an estimated 100 billion to 200 billion galaxies in the observable universe, but the actual number could be much higher.
  • Light from the Sun takes about 8 minutes to reach Earth.
